Our Goals

  • Provide alternative and direct employment in aquaculture sector.

  • Provide markets for fisheries by catch and processing by-products.

  • Provide markets for Zimbabwean farmers of soya, maize and other cereals.

  • Reduce pressure on wild capture fisheries.

  • Hands-on training environment and access to high quality faster growing aqua feed and technology for small scale farmers.

  • Offer Consultancy services through contract management and mentorship

  • To be the leading supplier of key quality inputs in the aqualculture industry in Zimbabwe.
